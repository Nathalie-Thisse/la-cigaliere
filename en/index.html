La Cigalière Hotel

Surrounded by a large pine forest, La Cigalière offers accommodation near the famous Bandol vineyards, a 15-minute drive from the sea. It has an outdoor pool, a petanque court and a picnic area.

The rooms offer a view of the private garden. They include a private bathroom with toiletries.

A buffet breakfast is served every morning, and you can enjoy it on the shaded terrace in the garden.

Private parking is available free of charge on site to allow you to easily visit Provence by car. Toulon train station is a 25-minute drive away, and the medieval village of Castellet is only 6 km away. The Paul Ricard racetrack, meanwhile, is 10 km away.
